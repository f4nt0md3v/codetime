О code_time

As a network of software professionals, we work independently but with the support of the group. You may hire one of us or many of us, but you always get the wisdom of many.

How can we help you?
  • we code

    We build solid, yet flexible solutions to maximize product value.

  • we learn

    Our hands-on approach to taking on everyday challenges differentiates us in our industry.

  • we share

    Through our presentations and workshops, we draw on and add to the wisdom and expertise of the community.

Everyone in the network
shares our work values:
  • Trustworthy
  • Good communicators
  • Eternal apprentices
  • Independent and self-organized
